Our Story

Tutors246 was founded out of a love for education and providing educational services, which are student centered. We believe that all students have the ability to excel and to achieve their full potential by working with their interests and learning pace. Our tutors are patient, friendly and trained in student centered approaches. We have a proven track record of helping both young learners and adults improve both their comprehension and application of knowledge in various subjects.


Our Process

At Tutors246, we start our tutoring program with a free assessment that is designed to determine your performance in the subject area in three main areas:

  1. Comprehension
  2. Knowledge/Understanding
  3. Application of Knowledge

Following the assessment, a customized tutoring plan would be designed which takes into account your current learning level and pace on one hand, and the syllabus and timeline on the other. This ensures that the tutoring plan delivers optimal instruction to enable you, the student, to achieve academic success.

Each session will follow a module in the syllabus with the majority of the sessions focusing on the modules which you need more assistance with, based on the initial assessment and progress test.
